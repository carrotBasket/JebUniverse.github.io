-Backstory-

Beginning

There used to be only one character: JEB. At the time, Jeb was only just a fish with a

party hat that had legs, but later, he evolved into something MUCH more...

Then came Pickle Penguin. Pickle Penguin (PP) wanted to take over the world.

Thankfully, Notch came to the rescue. Of course, Notch cooked PP. But suddenly, a new

enemy approached. His name? KITTY. Before he could do any damage, though, Notch just

fell on him, and that was the end of that.
